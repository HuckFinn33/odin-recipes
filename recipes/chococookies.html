Chocolate Chip Cookies

Fresh cookies

This homemade recipe for chocolate chip cookies is just like your grandmother made

Gather the following ingredients into appropriately sized mixing bowls: add the dry ingredient into one bowl and the eggs and melted or softened butter into another. Make sure to combine ingredients and mix well.

Ingredients

    1 cup butter, softened 1 cup white sugar 1 cup packed brown sugar 2 eggs 2 teaspoons vanilla extract 2 teaspoons baking soda 2 teaspoons hot water 1/2 teaspoon salt 3 cups all-purpose flour 2 1/4 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
