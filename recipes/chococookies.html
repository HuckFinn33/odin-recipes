Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This homemade recipe for chocolate chip cookies is just like your grandmother made. You won't believe how they melt in your mouth. With a ten minute prep time and ten more minutes in the oven, you will taste the best cookies you've ever had. This recipe was passed down from generation to generation in someones family. A random marketer somehow got a hold of it, and now it's posted online for the world to see...and hopefully eat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/4 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
    Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
    Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.
    Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  3. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
